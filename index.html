Guidelines

Server Rules

Please read and follow these rules to ensure a great experience for everyone

01

Respect All Players

Treat all players with respect regardless of their background. Harassment, discrimination, and toxic behavior will not be tolerated. This includes voice chat, text chat, and Discord interactions.
02

No Random Deathmatch (RDM)

Do not attack or kill other players without valid roleplay reason. All hostile actions must have proper roleplay context and initiation. Random violence ruins the experience for everyone.
03

No Vehicle Deathmatch (VDM)

Using vehicles as weapons to injure or kill other players is strictly prohibited. Vehicles should be used for transportation only, not as tools for combat unless in specific approved scenarios.
04

Value Your Life

Always value your character's life. If you're at gunpoint or in a dangerous situation, react realistically. Don't perform unrealistic actions that your character wouldn't do in real life.
05

No Metagaming

Do not use out-of-character information for in-character purposes. Information from Discord, streams, or other external sources cannot be used in roleplay unless your character learned it in-game.
